Program Overview

Business Technology Management is a program that focuses on combining business principles with ICT-based innovation. The program includes an optional co-op, case studies, and utilizes state-of-the-art classrooms and labs. It emphasizes analyzing business needs, designing technology-based processes and solutions, and effectively communicating these. The aim is to prepare students to anticipate, capitalize on, and drive business strategies and opportunities tied to technological change
