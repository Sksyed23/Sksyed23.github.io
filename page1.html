Thorncliffe Park

About Thorncliffe Park

Thorncliffe Park is a neighborhood defined as much by its vibrant community spirit as by its geographic location. Nestled within the bustling cityscape, it's a place I've proudly called home my entire life.

Over 80% of its residents are immigrants, refugees, and individuals from low-income households. This rich tapestry of cultures and backgrounds, however, has faced challenges. Inequities in accessibility and treatment often mean that these community members don't always receive the opportunities they deserve.

My Community Work

Dedicated to erasing these disparities, I've devoted much of my time and effort to the community that raised me. Recognizing the power of collective action, I founded a grassroots initiative, SAVETPARK. This movement arose from the ground up, serving as a conduit for the community's voice—a voice striving to be heard, acknowledged, and empowered.

Through SAVETPARK, I've aimed to shine a light on the concerns that matter most to my neighbors and to advocate for change that respects and reflects the community's needs.

My Personal Mission

My commitment to Thorncliffe Park extends to the vital community hubs that offer solace and support. Working with food banks and local mosques, I've immersed myself in service, seeking to strengthen the social fabric that weaves us all together.

Whether it's ensuring families have enough to eat or nurturing the spiritual well-being of our residents, my work is driven by a desire to build bridges—bridges to a future where the youth of Thorncliffe Park can aspire to success and claim their rightful place alongside their peers across the city.

For me, this is not just about giving back; its about moving forward, hand in hand, as a community united. It is about forging paths for our youth, so that they too can contribute to and revel in the kind of world that views each individual's potential as a collective triumph. My journey is rooted here, in the heart of Thorncliffe Park, where every effort to uplift my neighbors is a step towards a more inclusive and equitable society.
